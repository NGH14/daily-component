/Bún Bò Huế/

Bún Bò Huế is a simple beef noodle soup from Vietnam a spicy beef noodle soup from central Vietnam's imperial city of Hue characterized by its lemongrass-infused broth, fermented shrimp paste (mắm ruốc), pig's blood cubes, thick rice vermicelli, and distinctive balance of spicy, sour, salty, and sweet flavors that sets it apart from regular Pho.
