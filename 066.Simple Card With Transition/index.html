Vietnamese Cuisine

Bun Bo

The dish has a mix of spicy, salty, and savory flavors. The predominant flavors are those of lemongrass and shrimp paste.

Banh Mi

A typical Vietnamese roll or sandwich is a fusion of meats and vegetables from native Vietnamese cuisine such as chả lụa (Vietnamese sausage), coriander (cilantro), etc.

Banh Cuon

The name literally means rolled because these delicious little treats are a batter of primarily rice flour, and the traditional filling is made of pork, onion, jicama, and mushrooms which are then rolled up.
